# HONEY CAKE 
How to make honey cake 

Ingredients 

 for eggless cake:
▢¾ cup (200 grams) curd / yogurt
▢1 cup (230 grams) sugar
▢¾ cup (135 grams) oil
▢1 tsp vanilla extract
▢2 cup (330 grams) maida / plain flour
▢¼ tsp baking soda
▢1 tsp baking powder
▢½ cup (120 grams) milk, if required
for honey syrup:
▢2 tbsp sugar
▢¼ cup water
▢¼ cup honey
for jam mixture:
▢½ cup strawberry jam
▢1 tbsp honey
